Bandenkick

Bandenkick is an esport-league-service, specialized in eFootball / eSoccer.

I had and still have the honor of programming their website, which has continued to grow over the last few years. From 10 to 10,000 hits per day.

The website was developed without large frameworks to give full control over every little detail. Although this is very time-consuming, it was worth it.

The backend of the website is based on PHP and MySQL is used for the database.
